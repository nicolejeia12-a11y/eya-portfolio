A life with dim — April 28, 2026

there are days where the light feels different. softer. like someone put a lavender filter over reality. those are the days i think about dim the most — how someone can just exist in your life and rearrange everything without even trying...

[ read more ↓ ]

i started listening to seventeen differently now. songs that used to just sound good now sound like something i needed to hear. i think that's what it means to have someone change the way you experience art — they become part of every beautiful thing you encounter after.

anyway. dim. you're annoying. i love that about you. goodnight 🖤

thoughts at 3am and why aesthetics matter — April 10, 2026

it's 3am and i'm in a Pinterest spiral again. there's something about the way a carefully curated image board can feel like a whole mood — like a frequency you tune into...

[ read more ↓ ]

i think aesthetics are how some of us process emotion. when i'm sad i make things dark and grainy and beautiful. when i'm hopeful everything gets soft and golden and a little blurry at the edges. it's not shallow — it's a visual diary.

okay it's 4am now. i should sleep. i won't. goodnight 🌙

why i made this site look like a 2006 MySpace — May 1, 2026

everyone's portfolio looks the same. white background. clean layout. and they all look... fine. fine is boring. i wanted mine to feel like something...

[ read more ↓ ]

so i made it look like my brain: chaotic, pink, full of stars, a little too much. the blinking things. the spinning ★. the music player that doesn't actually play music. it's honest. it's me.

if someone doesn't get it, we probably weren't compatible anyway. ★
